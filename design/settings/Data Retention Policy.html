Data Retention Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

This policy outlines how we handle and retain your personal data to provide you with our meal tracking and personalized meal plan generation services. We are committed to ensuring your data is handled responsibly and transparently.

1. Data We Collect and Retain

We collect and retain the following types of data to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile information.
  • Health & Activity Data: Weight, height, age, gender, dietary preferences, allergies, fitness goals, and tracked meals.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, feature usage, and device information.

2. Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. This allows us to provide you with a continuous experience and maintain your meal history and preferences.

  • If your account is inactive for a period of 24 consecutive months, we may anonymize your data by removing personal identifiers.
  • Anonymized data may be retained for statistical and research purposes to improve our services.

3. Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and associated personal data at any time. You can do this through the 'Delete Your Account' option in the Privacy Settings.

  • When you delete your account, we will permanently and irrevocably delete your personal data from our active systems within 30 days.
  • Backup copies of your data may be retained for up to 90 days in our backup archives, after which they will be permanently deleted.

4. Policy Updates

We may update this Data Retention Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through an in-app notification.
